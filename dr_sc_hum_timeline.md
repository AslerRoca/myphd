## Hard deadlines, in one place

- **≥ 3 weeks** before a committee meeting — application / dissertation must arrive.
- **≤ 6 months** after acceptance — hold the 1st TAC meeting.
- **≥ 3 years** between acceptance and thesis submission.
- **≥ 4 weeks** before the defence — notify the Promotionsbüro of date and place.
- **≤ 1 year** after the oral exam — fulfil the publication obligation (or rights lapse).

## Things easy to forget

- Candidacy starts on the committee's **decision date**, not when the letter arrives — start working early.
- Keep your **own copies** of every TAC report; you need the full set to open the examination procedure.
- Original signatures, ordinary (non-recycled) paper, unstapled pages — at every submission.
- The **Votum informativum** is sealed: you carry it but never open it.
- You may **not** use the doctoral title until the Urkunde is in hand.
