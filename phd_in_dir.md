My journey to PhD start from Heilbronn, a small city 60 km from Heidelberg, but take 1 to 3 hours to arrive with DB. In Heilbronn, I finished my Master thesis under Dr. Christoph Maier's supervision. The thesis was trying to use convLSTM solve the time sequence problem on prediction the next frame on Echophegeography. Dr. Maier is a nice person, he motivated me to go into research area and teached me on critical thinking.


After I decided to continue my research path, I was seeking for one PhD position. That was the moment DIR came in my eyes. So I applied, and got the recommendation from Dr. Maier, passed though the interview, and ready to start my PhD.


Of course I had no clu about anything to start the PhD, the Dr. sc. hum. There is promotions ordnung, website for description, but not in a easy format to understand. That is the motivation for this draft. I hope this will make the Dr. sc. hum. progress more clearly.


So first, I need a doctor supervisor. It was lucky that Prof. Dr. med. Mark Oliver Wielputz had an interested idea in related to AI: use AI score chest X-ray using visual MRI scores as ground truth. After I had a supervisor and thema, we still need two more people in the TAC-Commitee. The request of other two members now is: they must hold a PD. or Prof. and to the best, not in the same department with you. The suggestion here will be, find the people who is intension on research, and has clear way to contact, response fast. On this thema, you need to ask help from the supervisor and all the other college. You will need your supervisor to aid you prepare the most two important documents are application, aka Promotionsvereinbarung, and a project description with background, methods, objects and so on, because you know nothing about the topic you are going to get in. The project description is a little tricky. To my experimence, 2 pages are the best scale. Do not write the methodology too short, or you will have trouble.


There is a check list online tell you what kind of documents I need, so I collected all the documents, putted them into one big evolop (this will happen again and again), and bring it to promotionsoffice and though it into the mailbox under building INF670. The thing after happens as it is, what I can do was just wait for the judgement.


The submitted filles will be checked by one sekterien in promotionsoffice. If everthing in ordnung, then your document will be putted on the table of next promotion commitee meeting. If something missing or need to be changed, you will be contacted by the sekterien, and that is the reason why the submission of documents need to be at least 3 weeks before the commitee meething's appointments. Those appointments can be found online, usually will start every 2 month, but some times every 3 month. 


During the time of waiting, the work can already be started. From the submission to recive of decision, the time frame is usually 3 month. But the time point you became a PhD candidate is not the time you recive the decision, but the time point they made the decision, aka the time they have the meeting. So why weast those times, right?


If the decision is negativ, then edit the application documents as requirement, and submit another application in next appointment. If decision is positive, then, congratulation, you started your next phase, the study and research phase. In this second phase, there are two points need to be explained: the TAC-Meeting and the Blue-Sheet.


If you got accepted as a PhD candidate, you will need to registry as a student in University Heidelberg, unless you have a equal or more thant 50% contract with university or hospital, in that case you can apply for the registry free. But at the sametime, you will not get your student card which gives you discount in mensa if you do not registry as a student.


There should be at least 3 TAC-Meeting, or 4 when needed. Before every TAC meeting, you should send the TAC member the description of the content report. During the TAC meeting, the TAC members need to score and comment you on the official report file, and in the end signed on your report. The TAC meeting can be orgnised online, then the signatures need to be collected after the meeting. After every TAC meeting, both the description and the report need to be scanned to send to the emial (TODO). Do not though them after scan, you will need them after your last TAC meeting to open the promotion process. The TAC meetings have its role.


The 1st TAC meeting is the openning of your thema, show the raw data you collected, and the analysis of the rawdata. The first TAC meeting need to be orgnised in the first year, at the best first 6 months, after you got accepted as a PhD Candidate. Do you still remember which date you should take to caculate the timeframe?


The second TAC meeting is the middle report. There is no clear definition about when you should have that meeting, but should be orgnised in my mind after you have some really interested results to report.


And the thired TAC meeting is normaly the last TAC meeting. By the time you should aready have enough data to draft a thesis, and on that meeting, the TAC member will decide if you can start to write the thesis, or start to prepare to open the promotions process. And when I say promotions process, it refer to the time from you submitte your thesis until you have your defence sucessfully been done. Of course that will be another story.


In one sentence, TAC meetings are the official meetings must be documented, beside that you may need to orgnise more meeting except that meetings for problem solving, small progress report and so on.

After your last TAC meeting, it is time to prepare to open the promotion process. You can either choose cumlative promotion, which you published 2 or more peer reviewed paper, then you do not need to write the thesis, but combine them together. This part I will leave to Claudia to write. 


the final note of your PhD is related to the number and quality of publications you published. Normally one top 30 percent paper mean magna cum laude, two or more papers means Summa cum laude, no paper is cum laude. You can add the condition to say if you get your paper published 30 days before your defence, you can still get the magna cum laude or summa cum laude. If your thesis has been scored as summa cum laude, then they will ask another 3 additional professor to score your thesis.


For the thesis, there is one Latex based Templet from DKFZ, that is the one I used. You may want to check university bibliothek, and to take a look how it looks like. The point is to make the structure clear and explain others what you did. No worry about this step, because your results have been supervised under TAC members, and you should have enough data to write the thesis. After your supervisor gives you the green light to your thesis, and you have all the other material for submission, you need to print two thesis example for reviewer. The documents from your side are prepared, package them then. But before you go to though them into that mail box under the building, you still need the erst gutachten from your supervisor. 


The process of erst gutachten is something like this: your supervisor need to prepare it, and send it to the sekterien, for us in Masillius Arkaden Frau Katja Schroder. You will need to take the gutachten from Katja, in one closed evelope. The PhD candidate is not allowed to see the document, but need to bring it with all the document together to promotions office and though in to that mail box. There is one hard rule for the submission of your thesis is: the minimum time between yor acception as PhD candidate until your submission of theis is at least 3 years.


After that is another silence until promotions office inform you about the result of the decision. You will recive one email to confirm that your thesis has been scored, and quality is good enough. In that E-mail, one defence coordinator and three final examer will be listed. In those three examer, the main examer is your doctor supervisor, and anohter two side examer will be probably those two you nominated. Then you need to orgnise the defence, make a slot query let them choose. To be noticed, the final defence date must be informed to the promotionoffice 4 weeks before that date.


If some one want a remote defence, like me, which is not recommendend, here is what will happen. First you will need to write email to the sekterien in promotionoffice to explain them why you want the defence be online. After you get confirmation, you need to nominate one professor or a PD with his/her resume to promotionsoffice. That professor/PD you nominated must stay with you in the same room when the defence happen. And the online defence can only be find though microsoft team, webex meeting or Heiconf. That is complex, and only meaningful if you have to do it. Or a offline defence will be a better choice. Me as example, I was in China, and orgnised everything, we had the defence. After all, the monitor (professor in china) must confirm something with signature, and send it back to germany per post or Fax (these are only two way they accept, or he need to bring it here personally).


And remember, the defence is one exam, not only presentation about your thesis. The examers will ask you questions, so suggestion will be to contact your examer before your defence, and ask for more details. Good luck to your defence!
